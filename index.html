Security posture

We run the same approach we use internally: the OpenClaw gateway binds to loopback, traffic goes through a Cloudflare Tunnel, and access is protected by Cloudflare Access. That means no open inbound ports on your VPS.

  • Cloudflare Access login (email/OTP or SSO)
  • Audit logs available
  • No exposed dashboard port
  • Secrets stored locally with strict permissions

Cost control

Most “surprise bills” come from unclear model usage. We keep model choices predictable and we can add usage monitoring. You pay your AI provider directly (BYOK), so there’s no markup and no hidden fees.

FAQ

Do I need technical skills?

No. You’ll follow a short onboarding checklist and we handle the server-side setup. We guide you through API keys and bot creation.

Do you include AI model costs?

No. You bring your own AI provider keys (OpenRouter/OpenAI/etc.) and pay them directly. That keeps costs transparent.

How do I access the dashboard?

You get a link protected by Cloudflare Access. No open ports on the VPS. Login can be email/OTP or SSO.

Which channels are supported?

Telegram is the default. Discord is optional. WhatsApp depends on the exact setup and is confirmed during onboarding.

Can I move away later?

Yes. You can export configuration and data. No lock-in. The VPS and keys are yours.

What’s included in the care plan?

Updates, monitoring, backup checks, and small changes within clear limits. If you need custom automation, we scope it separately.

How fast is delivery?

Typical setup is 24–48 hours after onboarding details are received.

Do you offer WhatsApp officially?

We can support it in some cases, but we won’t promise it as “standard” until we can guarantee a simple, repeatable setup.
